About Canine Club House

Canine Club House is a local, owner-operated dog daycare, boarding, and training facility in Perry, Georgia. Our team treats each dog like family and focuses on safe play, clear communication, and lots of love.

Safe, Structured, and Fun

From first-timers to regulars, every dog is introduced thoughtfully and monitored throughout the day. Playgroups are designed around size, personality, and play style, so dogs can relax and just be dogs.

Family-Owned & Community-Focused

We’re proud to serve Perry and the surrounding areas. Many of our clients drive in from out of town because they trust the care, communication, and consistency their dogs receive here.
